Smart blood pressure measurement    （Products in kind prevail）

1.The tester sitting upright，Calm。

2.After put instrument on arm，Fixed above the elbow.1-2CM，Palm upward，Air duct is located inside。

3.Press“Standby/measure”，Time and blood pressure units。

4.Wait a second，The machine began to sound.，A numerical test is done on the TV and the display.。

5.Press“Standby/measure”Close blood pressure gauge，According to the“OK”Button to return to the main menu。

Smart blood glucose measurement    （Products in kind prevail）

1.Clean and dry hands。

2.Insert the test paper into the blood glucose meter。

3.Blood taking pen，After the test paper is filled with blood，Instruments enter into the countdown state，Numerical measurements on the display and TV。

4.Take out test paper of blood glucose meter，According to the“OK”Buttonto confirm，Return to main menu
