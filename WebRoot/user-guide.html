Blood pressure    （The image shown here is indicative only. The actual product may differ.）

1.Sit up straight and relax.

2.Put cuff above elbow 1-2 CM.

3.Press “Standby/Measure” button to start.

4.The cuff will be inflated and results will displayed on TV.

Blood Glucose    （The image shown here is indicative only. The actual product may differ.）

1.After washing your hands, insert a test strip into your meter.

2.After washing your hands, insert a test strip into your meter. 2.Use your lancing device on the side of your fingertip to get a drop of blood.

3.Touch and hold the edge of the test strip to the drop of blood, and wait for the result.

4.Your blood glucose level will appear on the meter's display and TV.
